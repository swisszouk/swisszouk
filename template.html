How to add a party?

Please send me an email at zouk@ptak.me. Use the following template: 

title: Bürklizouk
location: Bürkliplatz
date: 2022-09-23
time: 18:00
price: 0
URL: https://example.com/event

Attach a square logo of your event (just the logo, not a poster). I will try and add the event within 48 hours.
